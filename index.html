ABOUT US:

Pizza Paradise is a gourmet neighborhood pizzeria located in the heart of New York City. Owners Billy and Jessica opened Pizza Paradise in 1998 with the hopes of creating a lively gathering space where great food, community, and culture could intersect.

While the menu features simple classic Italian choices, its flavors are anything but basic, as each pizza features its own unique crust, distinct sauce and amazingly fresh toppings. Rumor has it that Pizza Paradise also boasts a secret pie unlisted from its menu, sure to surprise and delight consumers who inquire about it.

At Pizza Paradise, we pride ourselves on preparing a delicious meal for every customer, every time. Our ingredients are fresh, and our staff is friendly and courteous. Check us out and let us show you that our customer's satisfaction is our number one priority!
